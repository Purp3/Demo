01. n!help
Shows all commands

02. n!script
Sends you the script

03. n!buy
Sends you a purchase link with instructions

04. n!redeem
Redeems your purchase

05. n!avatar
Shows the avatar

06. n!activate
Activates your licence key to use nexus

07. n!uid-reset
Resets your nexus user account

08. n!verify
Verifies new users to the server

09. n!check-version
Shows available versions

10. n!add-resell
Gives you a resell role and access to a support channel

11. n!remove-resell
Removes the resell role

12. n!add-offer
Shows available offers
